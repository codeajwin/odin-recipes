Biriyani

Description

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originated in South Asia, with variations and adaptations found across the Indian subcontinent, the Middle East, and beyond.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Whole spices (bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon)
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Garam masala
  • Onion
  • Biryani masala powder
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken
  2. Prepare the Rice
  3. Cook the Chicken
  4. Biryani Masala Layer
  5. Layering and Garnishing
  6. Dum Cooking
  7. Serve
