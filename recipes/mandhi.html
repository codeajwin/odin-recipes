Mandhi

Description

Mandi is a traditional Yemeni dish known for its unique flavor

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Black pepper
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Garam masala
  • Onion
  • Mandi sauce
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken
  2. Prepare the Rice
  3. Cook the Chicken
  4. Layering and Garnishing
  5. Dum Cooking
  6. Baking
  7. Serve
