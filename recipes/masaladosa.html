Masala Dosa

Description

Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian dish known for its crispy texture and flavorful potato filling

Ingredients

  • Parboiled rice
  • Urad dal
  • Fenugreek seeds
  • Potatoes
  • Grated coconut
  • Green chilies
  • Roasted chana dal
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Prepare Dosa Batter
  2. Make Potato Filling
  3. Prepare Coconut Chutney
  4. Make Masala Dosa
  5. Serve
